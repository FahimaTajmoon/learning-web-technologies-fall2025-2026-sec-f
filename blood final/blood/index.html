How Blood Bridge Works

Simple steps to connect donors with recipients in need

1

Register & Create Profile

Sign up as a donor, recipient, or organization. Provide necessary details including blood type and location.

2

Request or Offer Blood

Recipients can request specific blood types. Donors can mark themselves as available for donation.

3

AI Matching & Notification

Our AI matches requests with suitable donors based on proximity and compatibility. Both parties get notified.

4

Connect & Donate

Use our secure chat system to coordinate. After donation, records are stored on blockchain for transparency.

5

Earn Rewards

Donors earn points for each successful donation which can be redeemed for rewards.

About Blood Bridge

Blood Bridge is a comprehensive web and mobile-based blood donation management system developed as part of the Web Technologies course at American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB).

The platform addresses critical challenges in traditional blood donation systems by integrating donors, recipients, organizations, and administrators into one unified platform.

Using Agile Scrum methodology, our team is developing this solution with cutting-edge technologies including AI for donor matching and blockchain for secure record-keeping.
